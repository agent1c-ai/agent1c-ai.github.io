Delete Agent1c Android Account

Last updated: February 27, 2026

To request deletion of your Agent1c Android account and associated data, email privacy@agent1c.ai from the same email account used to sign in.

Required email subject

Delete my Agent1c account

What to include

  • Your sign-in email or social username (X/Google).
  • Approximate last login date.

Processing time

Deletion requests are processed within 30 days.

Data retention notes

  • Account-linked profile/session records are removed.
  • Limited security/abuse logs may be retained for up to 30 days.
